Q. Can I just book and join a trek?

Yes, with us it is possible to book a trek and directly join your group. You are not required to worry about all the formalities. We will do that on behalf of you.

Q. How to choose a trek?

It is very simple to choose a trek, you can either search by the trek name and compare the trek among the operators or with other treks. Second option is more suitable if you don’t know the name of the trek or you are not sure. You just have to search by region, season and number of days. We would give you the whole list from which you can compare and select the trek that suits you the best.

Q. What are the fitness requirements?

Each trek has its own fitness requirements, based on the difficulty level. In general, easy treks require minimal fitness, moderate trek requires active physical participation in daily life and hard trek requires more stamina and mental fitness. More details are available in the detail information of each trek.

Q. What gear must I bring for a Himalayan trek?

When you’re packing for a high-altitude trek, there are a few basic things you need – your warm layers, your trekking shoes, a good backpack and your toilet and medical kits. Other trek specific details are available in detail information of each trek.

Q. How do I register?

.Click on the “Register Now” button on any trek page to view availability and register online.
.Registering for a trek is an entirely online process. Once you choose the dates that suit you, our system will guide you through the process.
.You will be able to pay using your credit card / debit card/ net banking and other standard online payment options.

Q. What’s your cancellation policy?

We do not provide our own treks, so the cancellation policy is as per the trekking service provider’s policy. It may vary between the providers. We request you to go through the cancellation policy of each trek before booking on our platform.
